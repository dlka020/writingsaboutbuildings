CV

Dongjoo LEE, artistic researcher.

MA Candidate in Art Theory

Korea National University of Arts, Seoul

I study in the field of art, architecture and design with contemporary artists and designers in Seoul, responding to history of western art, architectural theory, media culture and sci-fi. Typically, when I'm curating the exhibition, I design a space, manage events and PRs for artists and teach participants. Recently, I'm writing my thesis for master degree at K-Arts Art theory.
