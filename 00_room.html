A ROOM

문의 공간 문의 장면, 2017.

Door is a fundamental element in space. It could have a variety of materials and represent a signal of beginning in a space. Depending on scale, shape and material, it has typical, specific and poetic traits itself, which gives those things to users of a space. Change, variation, seperation happen in space when people touch the door.
